Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM)
Overview

SMAM promotes farm mechanization with focus on small & marginal farmers, custom hiring centres (CHCs), and training/testing infrastructure. It provides financial assistance for selected equipment and supports dissemination.

Key elements

Subsidy for individual purchases and CHCs; rates and eligible implements vary by year/state.

Special provisions often exist for SC/ST/women/small & marginal farmers.

Emphasis on custom hiring to enhance access for smallholders.

Practical steps

Identify eligible machines and authorized vendors.

Determine whether the state provides front-end subsidy or reimbursement and assist farmer with the process.

Promote CHCs for costly equipment to lower per-farm costs.

Notes & verification

Verify current list of eligible implements, subsidy rates and application process on state agriculture/SMAM pages.
