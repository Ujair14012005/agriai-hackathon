Alluvial Soil (गाळाची माती)

Found in river valleys, this soil is very fertile and rich in potash and lime. It's one of the most productive agricultural soils in India.

  • Ideal for: Grains, Vegetables, Sugarcane
  • Tip: Soil testing is recommended to manage nutrient levels effectively.
