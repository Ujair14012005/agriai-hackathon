PM-KISAN Scheme
Benefits:

Financial benefit of â‚¹6,000 per year is provided to eligible farmer families, payable in three equal 4-monthly installments of â‚¹2,000 each.

Eligibility:

All landholding farmers' families, who have cultivable landholding in their names are eligible.

How to Apply:

Farmers can self-register through the official PM-KISAN portal or approach the local revenue officer (Patwari) or a Nodal Officer nominated by the State Government.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
Benefits:

Provides comprehensive insurance coverage against crop loss to stabilize the income of farmers. It covers all stages of the crop cycle from pre-sowing to post-harvest.

Premium:

Farmers pay a very low premium: 2% for all Kharif crops, 1.5% for all Rabi crops, and 5% for commercial and horticultural crops. The remaining premium is paid by the Government.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme
Benefits:

Provides farmers with a revolving credit facility. It covers various needs such as cultivation costs, post-harvest expenses, and consumption requirements. The interest rates are also subsidized.

Soil Health Card Scheme
Benefits:

A printed report card is issued to farmers once every 2 years for all their land holdings. It helps farmers to improve soil health and increase productivity by applying nutrients based on recommendations.

How it Works:

Soil samples are tested in labs for 12 parameters. Based on the results, a detailed report is generated which is then used to provide advice to the farmer.
